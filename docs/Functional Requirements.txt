Functional Requirements
Category
Requirement ID
Requirement Description
Authentication & Profile
FR-01
Allows students to register, verify their email via a 6-digit OTP, and log in with a JWT token valid for 30 days.


FR-02
Provide options for the student to reset their password and update their profile.
Team Management
FR-03
Allow a student to create a team, becoming the Team Leader, or join an existing team via invitation.


FR-04
The Team Leader shall be able to invite up to 3 members via email and lock the team once the roster is finalized.
Project Initiation
FR-05
The system shall allow students to create a project title and description, which undergoes an automated Title Similarity Check (Levenshtein Distance).


FR-06
The system shall allow students to revise a title or submit a Title Change Request if high similarity is detected.
Capstone 1 (Proposal)
FR-07
The system shall provide an auto-template for Google Docs collaborative drafting (Chapters 1-3).


FR-08
The system shall allow students to upload chapter documents (DOCX/PDF/TXT, max 25MB) via Google Drive integration.


FR-09
The system shall require a late justification note if the submission is made past the deadline.
Capstone 2 (Development)
FR-10
The system shall track the status of system development (Design, Build, Test Modules).


FR-11
The system shall allow students to upload source code, system design documents, and test results for adviser review.
Revision & Defense
FR-12
The system shall allow students to view adviser/panel feedback and submit revised versions of their work.


FR-13
The system shall track the project status through phases: Proposed, Review, Approved for Defense, and Defended.
Concurrency & Integrity
FR-14
The system shall implement concurrency control to prevent duplicate submissions from different teammates at the same time (HTTP 409 Conflict).


FR-15
The system shall provide a Plagiarism Report showing similarity scores, matched passages, and source links.
Monitoring & Logs
FR-16
The system shall allow students to view a Project Progress Report, Submission History (with versioning), and a full Audit Trail/Logs.


FR-17
The system shall notify students of deadline alerts, approvals, revisions, and comments.
